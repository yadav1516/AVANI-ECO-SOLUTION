Frequently Asked Questions

How is solar panel maintenance done?

Simple cleaning is enough. Regular washing with water to remove dust keeps them operating at peak efficiency.

What happens during rain or cloudy weather?

Grid-tie system works seamlessly. When solar generation is low, your home automatically draws power from the grid.

In how many days does the subsidy arrive?

Approx 30-45 days after the installation and net-metering process is complete, provided all documentation is correct.
