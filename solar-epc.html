Renewable Energy for a Sustainable World.

Comprehensive Solar EPC Services

Turnkey Solutions for Solar Power Projects

At Avani Eco Solutions Pvt. Ltd., we deliver comprehensive Solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) services tailored to your specific energy goals. From initial feasibility studies to final commissioning, our expert team manages every aspect of your solar project, ensuring seamless execution, superior quality, and maximum return on investment.

Our Solar EPC Process

Engineering & Design

  • Customized Design: Tailored solar systems optimized for your site's specific geography and energy needs.
  • Advanced Engineering: Utilizing state-of-the-art software to maximize efficiency and energy yield.
  • Feasibility Studies: Comprehensive site assessment to ensure technical and financial viability.

Procurement

  • Tier-1 Sourcing: Direct procurement of high-efficiency panels and inverters from global leaders.
  • Quality Assurance: Rigorous checks to ensure all components meet the highest industry standards.
  • Cost Efficiency: Strategic partnerships enabling competitive pricing without compromising quality.

Construction & Installation

  • Expert Execution: Installation by certified professionals adhering to strict safety protocols.
  • Project Management: Dedicated managers ensuring on-time delivery and seamless coordination.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Full adherence to local building codes and safety regulations.

Commissioning & Testing

  • Rigorous Testing: Comprehensive performance analysis of modules, inverters, and connections.
  • Grid Synchronization: Seamless integration with existing power infrastructure.
  • Final Handover: Delivery of a fully operational plant with complete documentation and training.

Operation & Maintenance (O&M)

  • Long-term maintenance plans to keep your solar system running efficiently.
  • Remote monitoring services to track system performance in real-time.
  • Rapid response to any issues, ensuring minimal downtime and maximum energy production.
