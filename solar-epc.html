Comprehensive Solar EPC Services

Turnkey Solutions for Solar Power Projects

At Pareesan Services Pvt Ltd, we specialize in providing end-to-end solar EPC services to help you harness the power of the sun. Our team of experts ensures a seamless experience from the initial consultation to the final installation and beyond, delivering high-performance solar power systems tailored to your needs.

Our Solar EPC Process

Engineering & Design

  • Customized system design tailored to your specific energy requirements.
  • Advanced engineering solutions that maximize efficiency and energy yield.
  • Site assessment and feasibility studies to determine the best solar solution for your location.

Procurement

  • Sourcing high-quality solar panels, inverters, and other components from leading manufacturers.
  • Ensuring timely delivery of all materials to keep your project on schedule.
  • Competitive pricing and strong supplier relationships that offer you the best value.

Construction & Installation

  • Professional installation by certified technicians adhering to the highest safety and quality standards.
  • Project management from start to finish, ensuring smooth execution and timely completion.
  • On-site supervision to ensure all construction activities meet design specifications and regulatory requirements.

Commissioning & Testing

  • Comprehensive testing of all components to ensure optimal performance.
  • Grid connection and synchronization with existing power infrastructure.
  • Final inspection and commissioning to hand over a fully operational solar power system.

Operation & Maintenance (O&M)

  • Long-term maintenance plans to keep your solar system running efficiently.
  • Remote monitoring services to track system performance in real-time.
  • Rapid response to any issues, ensuring minimal downtime and maximum energy production.
