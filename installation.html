Expert Solar Installation & Balance of System (BoS) Services

Seamless Solar Installation with Precision and Care

At Avani Eco Solutions Pvt. Ltd., we provide top-tier solar installation and Balance of System (BoS) services designed for optimal performance and longevity. Our certified professionals ensure every project—residential, commercial, or industrial—is executed with military-grade precision, adhering to international safety and quality standards.

Our Installation Services Include:

Site Assessment & Preparation

  • Smart Analysis: Detailed shading and structural integrity analysis for optimal panel placement.
  • Site Engineering: Preparation of civil works and structural modifications to support the solar array.
  • Compliance Check: Ensuring all local zoning and building codes are met before work begins.

Panel Installation

  • Professional Mounting: Secure installation using storm-resistant clamps and structures.
  • Durable Connections: UV-rated and weather-proof cabling for long-term reliability.
  • Aesthetics: Clean cable management ensuring a neat visual appeal.

Inverter & Electrical Setup

  • Precision Components: Installation of high-efficiency inverters for maximum energy conversion.
  • Safe Wiring: Comprehensive electrical protection with DC/AC distribution boxes.
  • Grid Integration: Seamless connection to the grid or battery storage systems.

System Commissioning

  • Performance Testing: Validating voltage, current, and grounding parameters.
  • Monitoring Setup: Configuring remote monitoring for real-time performance tracking.
  • Client Handover: Full walkthrough and documentation for the system owner.

Comprehensive Balance of System (BoS) Solutions

The Balance of System (BoS) components are critical to the functionality and efficiency of your solar power installation. At Avani Eco Solutions Pvt. Ltd., we offer a complete range of BoS solutions, ensuring every aspect of your solar system is optimized for maximum performance.

BoS Components & Services Include:

Mounting Structures

  • Design and installation of durable and weather-resistant mounting systems for rooftop and ground-mounted installations.
  • Customizable solutions to suit various site conditions and panel types.

Electrical Components

  • High-quality wiring, connectors, and conduits to ensure safe and efficient power transmission.
  • Selection and installation of protective devices such as fuses, circuit breakers, and surge protectors.

Monitoring & Control Systems

  • Advanced monitoring solutions to track energy production and system performance in real-time.
  • Integration with smart home systems and mobile apps for easy access and control.

Energy Storage Solutions

  • Installation of battery storage systems to maximize energy independence and reliability.
  • Customized energy storage solutions tailored to meet your specific energy needs.

Safety Systems

  • Installation of safety equipment such as disconnect switches, fire protection, and grounding systems.
  • Adherence to all safety regulations and industry standards to protect your investment.
