Expert Solar Installation & Balance of System (BoS) Services

Seamless Solar Installation with Precision and Care

At Pareesan Services Pvt Ltd, we provide top-tier solar installation services designed to deliver optimal performance and long-term reliability. Our team of certified professionals ensures that every solar project, whether residential, commercial, or industrial, is installed with the utmost precision and care, adhering to the highest industry standards.

Our Installation Services Include:

Site Assessment & Preparation

  • Detailed site analysis to determine the best placement and orientation of solar panels.
  • Assessment of structural integrity and shading to optimize energy production.
  • Preparation of the site, including necessary civil works and structural modifications.

Panel Installation

  • Professional mounting of solar panels using industry-leading techniques.
  • Ensuring secure and durable connections that withstand environmental conditions.
  • Neat and efficient cable management for a clean, maintained pleasing installation.

Inverter & Electrical Setup

  • Expert installation of inverters, ensuring compatibility and maximum efficiency.
  • Connection to the grid or battery storage systems with all necessary wiring and protections.
  • Compliance with local electrical codes and standards to guarantee safety and performance.

System Commissioning

  • Thorough testing of the entire system to ensure optimal operation.
  • Integration with monitoring systems to track performance and energy output.
  • Final inspection and approval, handing over a fully operational solar energy system.

Comprehensive Balance of System (BoS) Solutions

The Balance of System (BoS) components are critical to the functionality and efficiency of your solar power installation. At Pareesan Services Pvt Ltd, we offer a complete range of BoS solutions, ensuring every aspect of your solar system is optimized for maximum performance.

BoS Components & Services Include:

Mounting Structures

  • Design and installation of durable and weather-resistant mounting systems for rooftop and ground-mounted installations.
  • Customizable solutions to suit various site conditions and panel types.

Electrical Components

  • High-quality wiring, connectors, and conduits to ensure safe and efficient power transmission.
  • Selection and installation of protective devices such as fuses, circuit breakers, and surge protectors.

Monitoring & Control Systems

  • Advanced monitoring solutions to track energy production and system performance in real-time.
  • Integration with smart home systems and mobile apps for easy access and control.

Energy Storage Solutions

  • Installation of battery storage systems to maximize energy independence and reliability.
  • Customized energy storage solutions tailored to meet your specific energy needs.

Safety Systems

  • Installation of safety equipment such as disconnect switches, fire protection, and grounding systems.
  • Adherence to all safety regulations and industry standards to protect your investment.
