How Hybrid Solar Works

  • Daytime

    Solar panels power your home and charge the batteries.

  • Night / Power Cut

    Batteries automatically power your home. Grid is used only if needed.

  • Excess Power

    Any extra energy generated is sent to the grid for credits (Net Metering).
