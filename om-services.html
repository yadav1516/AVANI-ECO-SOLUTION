Operations & Maintenance (O&M)

Ensuring Peak Efficiency and Longevity

A solar asset is only as good as its maintenance. Our comprehensive Operations & Maintenance (O&M) services guarantee your system performs at its best for decades. We combine advanced monitoring technology with expert field support to minimize downtime and maximize your energy yield.

Our O&M Services Include:

Performance Monitoring

  • 24/7 Surveillance: Cloud-based tracking of real-time generation data.
  • Predictive Analytics: Identifying potential issues before they impact production.
  • Instant Alerts: Immediate notification of any system downtime or anomalies.

Preventive Maintenance

  • Scheduled Service: Regular health checks to ensure all components are optimal.
  • Professional Cleaning: Specialized module cleaning to remove dust and debris.
  • Safety Inspections: Thermography and electrical testing to prevent fire hazards.

Repairs & Corrective Action

  • Rapid Response: Dedicated field teams ready to resolve faults quickly.
  • Spare Parts Mgmt: Leveraging our inventory for immediate component replacement.
  • Root Cause Analysis: Detailed investigation to prevent recurrence of issues.

Reporting & Documentation

  • Monthly Reports: Detailed generation, PR (Performance Ratio), and uptime statistics.
  • Maintenance Logs: Digital records of all site activities and interventions.
  • Compliance: Ensuring regulatory reporting requirements are fully met.

Warranty Management

  • Claims Handling: End-to-end coordination with manufacturers for replacements.
  • Tracking: Monitoring warranty timelines for inverters, panels, and structures.
  • Peace of Mind: We handle the paperwork so you don't have to.
