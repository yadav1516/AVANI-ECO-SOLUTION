Operations & Maintenance (O&M)

Ensuring Peak Efficiency and Longevity

A solar system's efficiency depends on proper maintenance and performance tracking. Our Operations & Maintenance (O&M) services ensure that your solar power system functions at peak efficiency throughout its lifespan. We offer comprehensive care packages tailored to your specific needs.

Our O&M Services Include:

Performance Monitoring

  • Real-time data tracking for system optimization.
  • Detailed analytics to identify underperforming modules or strings.
  • Instant alerts for any system faults or anomalies.

Preventive Maintenance

  • Regular system inspections to prevent issues before they arise.
  • Scheduled cleaning of solar panels to maintain maximum output.
  • Checking and tightening of electrical connections.

Repairs & Claims Management

  • Fast resolution of system faults to minimize downtime.
  • Expert handling of warranty claims with equipment manufacturers.
  • Replacement of faulty components with genuine parts.

Technical Documentation

  • Providing essential reports and system performance insights.
  • Maintaining detailed records of all maintenance activities and repairs.
  • Compliance reporting for regulatory authorities.

Warranty Management

  • Coordination with manufacturers for hassle-free warranty claims.
  • Tracking of warranty periods for all major system components.
  • Ensuring all terms and conditions of warranties are met.
