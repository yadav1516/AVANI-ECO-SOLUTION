Who We Are

Pioneering the Solar Revolution

Established in 2024, Avani Eco Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a premier Solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) company committed to powering a sustainable future. Headquartered in Lucknow, our reach extends across multiple states through a robust network of regional offices, ensuring top-tier solar services wherever you are.

We specialize in delivering end-to-end solar energy solutions across all sectors, including Residential, Industrial, and Commercial. From large-scale C&I (Commercial & Industrial) projects to customized rooftop installations, our expertise in solar panel installation and system design ensures maximum efficiency and long-term savings.

As a UPNEDA-registered company, we take pride in maintaining the highest standards of quality and compliance. We don't just install solar panels; we simplify the transition to green energy by providing complete subsidy assistance, helping our clients navigate government incentives with ease.

At Avani Eco Solutions, we believe in innovation, reliability, and excellence, making clean energy accessible and affordable for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ROI for a commercial solar plant?

Typically, commercial solar plants have an ROI of 3-4 years, after which the electricity generated is essentially free for the next 20+ years.

Do you provide financing options?

Yes, we have tie-ups with leading banks and NBFCs to provide easy financing options for solar projects.

What happens during a power cut?

Grid-connected systems shut down for safety. However, hybrid systems with battery backup can continue to power essential loads.

How much subsidy will I get?

You can get up to ?78,000 subsidy:

  • 1 kW: ?30,000
  • 2 kW: ?60,000
  • 3 kW & above: ?1,08,000 (Max)

Avani Eco Solutions helps you with the entire documentation to make it hassle-free.

Can solar power run heavy appliances like ACs?

Yes. A properly sized solar system can easily power ACs, refrigerators, and geysers. It seamlessly integrates with your existing connection to handle high loads during the day.
