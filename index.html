Who We Are

Pioneering the Solar Revolution

Pareesan Services Pvt. Ltd. is a solar-focused EPC company delivering end-to-end renewable energy solutions for commercial, industrial, and residential clients. Founded in 2017, we specialize in Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) of high-performance solar power systems designed for efficiency, reliability, and long-term value.

With over 8 years of experience, we provide complete solar solutions from system design and engineering to procurement, installation, commissioning, and operations & maintenance. By leveraging advanced solar technologies and industry best practices, we help businesses and communities transition to clean, cost-effective, and sustainable energy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ROI for a commercial solar plant?

Typically, commercial solar plants have an ROI of 3-4 years, after which the electricity generated is essentially free for the next 20+ years.

Do you provide financing options?

Yes, we have tie-ups with leading banks and NBFCs to provide easy financing options for solar projects.

What happens during a power cut?

Grid-connected systems shut down for safety. However, hybrid systems with battery backup can continue to power essential loads.
